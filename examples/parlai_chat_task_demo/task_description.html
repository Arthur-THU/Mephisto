Movie Recommendation in Conversations


What is this task?

In this task, you will be assigned a role, either a movie seeker or a movie recommender. The seeker will ask the recommender for recommendation, and the recommender should recommend at least 3 movies that the seeker accepts.



Critical Information!

The seeker will be given characteristic about preferences of movies. However, both the seeker and the recommender can't directly talk about the preferences, or the HIT will be rejected. The recommender should guess the preferences in the following way:

  1. The recommender directly recommends a movie (e.g., I think you can't miss $Avatar$!)
  2. The seeker asks about the information about the movie (e.g., What does the movie talk about?)
  3. The recommender talks about the movie (e.g., It tells that people on earth want to plunder resources on other planets and the aliens fight back.)
  4. The seeker accepts or rejects the recommendation based on the preferences (e.g., Can you recommend something else rather than science fiction movie?)
